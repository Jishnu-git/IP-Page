Role in The Project

Feature Extraction or Detection is a key concept in image processing, as these help the computer to find relation between images i.e find patterns in images. In images objects can be identified by its edges, corners, or flat surface. I am looking through the various image processing techniques which are used for feature detection and edge detections.

Completed Work

In the vast space of Image Processing there are several feature detection algorithms and different edge detection algorithm . As of now I went through Speeded-Up Robust Features (SURF) image processing technique which is used for local, similarity invariant representation and comparison of images.

Ongoing Work

Ongoing work on the project after the research of the feature detection techniques, is to compare the images to show the improvement in matching rates between images with and without the pre-processing .
