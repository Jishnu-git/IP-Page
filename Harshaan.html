Role in The Project

Feature detection is the process of computing the abstraction of the image information and making a local decision at every image point to see if there is an image feature of the given type existing in that point. Feature detection and image matching have been two important problems in machine vision and robotics, and their applications continue to grow in various fields. ORB and BRIEF are examples of such feature detection .In ORB, a rotation matrix is computed using the orientation of patch and then the BRIEF descriptors are steered according to the orientation. The descriptor BRIEF poorly performs if there is an in-plane rotation.My role in the project is to find few pre-processing techniques to improve the feature detection methods ORB and BRIEF and to provide higher matching rates for the techniques .Higher matching rates show the improvement caused by the pre-processing techniques to provide a better edge and improvement in the ORB and BRIEF methods.

Completed Work

Throughout the vast field of Feature Detection there exists multiple number of methods for feature detection .When it comes to pre-processing such images before feature detection ,the process involves a huge amount of research work to be completed about the methods and the process involved in these methods .So far ,I have completed the research work on ORB and BRIEF detection methods and tried various methods of pre-processing and their effects on such methods .These Pre-Processing methods help improve the feature detection and help us match and relate to the original image with higher success rates .

Ongoing Work

Ongoing work on the project after the research of the feature detection techniques, involves the comparison of images to show improvement in matching rates between images which were pre-processed and images which aren't pre-processed .
